The Challenge

Designing for Life-Critical Decisions

Clinicians were drowning in patient data across multiple systems. AI models generated excellent predictions — but the interfaces buried them in noise. A wrong interpretation could cost a life.

😰 Problems Found

  • Clinicians switched between 5+ tools per patient review
  • AI predictions shown as raw numbers — no context or confidence
  • Alert fatigue: 200+ daily alerts, 90% dismissed unread
  • No WCAG compliance — inaccessible to colour-blind users
  • Dense tables replaced insight — data without meaning

✅ Design Solutions

  • Unified patient view combining all signals in one screen
  • AI insights displayed as actionable recommendations with confidence %
  • Smart alert triage: only top 3 critical alerts surfaced
  • Full WCAG AAA compliance — tested with colour-blind clinicians
  • Progressive disclosure: summary first, depth on demand
