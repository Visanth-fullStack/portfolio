The Challenge

Learning That Feels Personal

Traditional e-learning showed everyone the same videos at the same pace. Students dropped out because content was either too easy, too hard, or simply irrelevant to their goals. AI could fix this — but nobody had designed for it well.

😔 Problems Found

  • 72% dropout rate at Week 2 — boredom and pace mismatch
  • No progress visibility — learners felt lost in long curricula
  • AI model generated great recommendations, never surfaced
  • Gamification felt forced and patronising
  • Mobile app was an afterthought — desktop content on small screen

🌱 Design Solutions

  • Dynamic "Your Path" — AI reorders curriculum daily based on performance
  • Visual progress map with milestones and unlockable content
  • Streak system + XP that rewards consistency, not just completion
  • Authentic badges tied to skill mastery tests, not just lesson clicks
  • Native mobile-first design with thumb-zone navigation
