The Challenge

Making Shopping Feel Personal Again

Generic e-commerce was failing users — product grids showed everyone the same items regardless of context, history, or intent. The brand had powerful AI models but buried them behind a 2010-era UI.

📉 Problems Found

  • 75% of users left without interacting with any recommendation
  • AI model outputs ignored — surfaced only on dedicated "deals" pages
  • No visual hierarchy — everything competed equally for attention
  • Mobile experience felt cramped and unintuitive
  • Add-to-cart was buried; checkout flow had 7 steps

📈 Design Solutions

  • AI "For You" rail — contextual, above-the-fold every page
  • Visual language hierarchy: hero → featured → catalogue
  • Smart product cards with AI context labels ("You viewed similar")
  • Mobile-first redesign with bottom-nav and thumb zones
  • One-tap checkout with smart form auto-fill
