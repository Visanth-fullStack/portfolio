The Challenge

The Problem Space

LLM-powered tools were technically impressive but felt cold, generic, and intimidating. Users struggled to form a mental model of AI capabilities, and teams couldn't customise experiences to their brand voice.

😤 Problems Identified

  • Chat interfaces feel transactional, not conversational
  • No visual feedback during AI "thinking" — users felt lost
  • Zero brand customisation — all tools looked identical
  • Prompt writing was intimidating for non-technical users
  • History and context were buried and hard to access

✅ Design Solutions

  • Warm, animated chat UI with expressive typing indicators
  • Live streaming responses with confidence signals
  • Brand-aware theming system with token overrides
  • Smart prompt suggestions and guided onboarding
  • Sidebar with semantic conversation clustering
