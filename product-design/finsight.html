The Challenge

Making Finance Legible

Retail investors were drowning in charts, jargon, and noise. AI models generated excellent signals — but the interface looked like a Bloomberg terminal built for institutions, not individuals.

📉 Problems Found

  • Interface overwhelmed new investors — 68% churned at day 3
  • AI forecasts shown as raw probability numbers with no context
  • 6-second average page load — data-heavy charts on mobile
  • No explainability — users didn't trust AI recommendations
  • Dark/light mode toggling broke multiple data visualisations

📈 Design Solutions

  • Progressive complexity: "Simple / Pro" view toggle
  • AI insights with plain language explanations and confidence bars
  • Performance-first charts: SVG sparklines instead of Canvas
  • "Why" button on every AI signal — transparent reasoning shown
  • Full native dark-mode design system, no toggle bugs
